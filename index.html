About Simril Solutions

Simril Solutions Ltd is an independent product consultancy led by Josh May — a product manager with around 20 years in the UK energy sector, a Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO) and an engineering degree from the University of Birmingham.

The company works with energy suppliers and utilities on discrete, outcome-focused engagements: senior product leadership, product discovery and strategy, and the delivery of complex regulated change. Engagements are scoped as defined pieces of work with clear deliverables.
